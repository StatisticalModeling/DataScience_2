2.1 Writing the regression equation with and inline R code

The least squares regression equation from regressing log10 of price onto log10 of sqft_living and bedrooms is written in Equation (2.1).

\[\begin{equation} \widehat{\text{log10(price)}} = 2.693 + 0.941 \cdot \text{log10(sqft_living)} -0.033 \cdot \text{bedrooms} \tag{2.1} \end{equation}\]
